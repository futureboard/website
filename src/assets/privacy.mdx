# Futureboard Marketplace Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026/06/30

This Privacy Policy explains how **Futureboard Marketplace** (“Futureboard”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you access or use Futureboard Marketplace, including our website, marketplace services, creator tools, account systems, payment integrations, APIs, downloads, and related services.

For the purposes of applicable data protection law, including Thailand’s Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) (the “PDPA”), Futureboard acts as the data controller of the personal information described in this Policy.

By using Futureboard Marketplace, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Marketplace.

---

## 1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Futureboard Marketplace and related services, including:

* Marketplace browsing and search
* User accounts and authentication
* Creator profiles and product listings
* Digital content downloads
* Purchases and payment-related workflows
* Reviews, ratings, comments, and support requests
* Security checks, malware scanning, and abuse prevention
* Marketplace APIs and developer tools

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, payment providers, hosting providers, analytics providers, or external services that are not controlled by Futureboard. Those services may have their own privacy policies.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect different types of information depending on how you use the Marketplace.

### 2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

* Name or display name
* Email address
* Username
* Profile image or avatar
* Password or authentication credentials, where applicable
* OAuth or third-party login identifiers
* Account settings and preferences
* Creator profile information

### 2.2 Creator Information

If you register as a Creator or publish Content, we may collect:

* Creator name or brand name
* Public profile information
* Product listings and metadata
* Uploaded files and related assets
* License information
* Support contact information
* Payment account connection status
* Tax or payout-related metadata where required
* Marketplace performance data, such as sales, downloads, refunds, and ratings

Some Creator information may be visible publicly on the Marketplace.

### 2.3 Marketplace Activity

When you use the Marketplace, we may collect:

* Pages viewed
* Search queries
* Product views
* Downloads
* Purchases
* Reviews and ratings
* Wishlist or saved items
* Content reports
* Support tickets
* Device and session activity
* Referral sources

### 2.4 Payment Information

Payments may be processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe, PromptPay, or other supported providers.

Futureboard does not store full payment card numbers unless explicitly stated and legally permitted. However, we may receive and store limited payment-related information, such as:

* Transaction ID
* Purchase history
* Payment status
* Refund status
* Chargeback or dispute status
* Payment provider customer ID
* Creator payout status
* Billing country or region
* Tax-related metadata where required

Payment providers may collect and process your payment information under their own terms and privacy policies.

### 2.5 Uploaded Content and Files

When Creators upload Content, we may collect and process:

* Uploaded files
* File names
* File size
* File type
* Version information
* Checksums or hashes
* Metadata
* Screenshots and preview assets
* Documentation files
* License files
* Security scan results

We may process uploaded files for hosting, delivery, moderation, malware scanning, compatibility checks, indexing, and Marketplace operation.

### 2.6 Device, Log, and Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical information, including:

* IP address
* Browser type and version
* Operating system
* Device type
* Language settings
* Time zone
* Referring URLs
* Pages visited
* API requests
* Crash logs
* Error logs
* Download logs
* Security logs
* Approximate location derived from IP address

This information helps us operate, secure, debug, and improve the Marketplace.

### 2.7 Communications

If you contact us, report a bug, submit a support request, file an intellectual property complaint, or communicate with Creators through the Marketplace, we may collect:

* Your message content
* Contact information
* Attachments
* Support history
* Related account and transaction information

### 2.8 Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not seek to collect special categories of sensitive personal data (such as health, religion, or biometric data) and ask that you do not submit such information through public listings, reviews, or support messages unless strictly necessary.
